If you have experienced or witnessed a cybercrime, please select the type of attack you are reporting from the list below and fill out the corresponding details.

  2. Step 2: Fill in the required details based on the attack type. Each form will ask for information relevant to the specific attack you are reporting, such as victim details, attacker details, and incident description.

  3. Step 3: Attach any evidence or screenshots, if available, that can help law enforcement understand the attack better. For example, if youâ€™re reporting a Phishing attack, include the link or email involved.

  4. Step 4: Review your information to make sure everything is accurate. The more details you provide, the easier it will be for authorities to act on the report.

  5. Step 5: Click the "Submit Report" button. Once submitted, your report will be reviewed by the relevant authorities, and you will be contacted if further action is needed.
